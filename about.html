About Us

Balkoins is a team of cryptocurrency advisors that have backgrounds in engineering, accounting, finance, and supply chain management. Our team is composed of four certified public accountants, an IBM blockchain certified engineer, and a lifelong entrepeneur. Based in Detroit, we believe in hard work, and nothing but the best service for our customers.

Why Us?

Here at Balkoins, we believe in educating others on cryptocurrencies. Through honest hard work, we want to provide the best experience for our customers, and make their dive into the market as seamless as possible. Each of us coming from humble backgrounds, and going through the bumpy road entering cryptocurrencies, we realize that there is a lot to learn, and a true lack of human-to-human interactions.

We want to be the ones you call for the simple issue you just cant seem to figure out. Or, shoot a quick text to our team, and we will help you immediately. We want to be the crypto-genius friend of yours, that can answer whatever you need, whenever you need.

What We Do

We understand there are a lot of amazing opportunities in this market. Balkoins brings together many aspects of the cryptocurrency space. We want to be your on-command service no matter what the issue could be. We provide services for novices, and experts alike. Allow us to help you with investing, mining, and everything in between.
