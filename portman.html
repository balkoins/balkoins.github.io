Portfolio Management

  • Flexible Storage

    Cryptocurrencies may be stored on cold wallets(safer) or hot wallets(riskier). When they are stored cold, it may be more time consuming when you request a withdrawal or trade, but they are much more secure. When they are stored hot, the funds are much more availible and ready to be moved, but are not as secure. The storage of your assets are completely customized by you. (ex: 75% hot 25% cold)

  • Pricing

    After a $100 setup fee, Balkoins only takes %10 of profits at the time of withdrawal.

  • User Control

    Your assets will be managed by us with your best interest in mind. We want to make you money. Nevertheless, you are in ultimate control of your funds, and may withdraw your money at any time. We will consider any input you give us and apply it to your portfolio, no questions asked.

  • Fees

    Everytime cryptocurrencies are bought, sold, or sent, fees are applied. Balkoins does not apply our own fees, these fees are a result of the exchanges or networks and are beyond our control. We will keep track of the fees paid from the transaction activity, so you know exactly where your money is going.

  • Transparency

    Balkoins believes in 100% transparency. You may request a portfolio report at any time. All transactions and trades are timestamped, and we will provide proof of transactions and balances upon request.
